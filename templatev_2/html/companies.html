Host Competitions

Competitions engage your customers by letting them co-create and co-develop new products and services that they want to pay for. You can even let them vote as a community on what ideas they like best. The uses for competitions are limited only by your creativity, so use them to evaluate new potential hires, marketing, and customer feedback.

"I use competitions to let my customers decide as a group what drink specials I should offer on certain nights. The 'wow-factor' of letting them help me make decisions like that has definitely transformed my bar into a local hotspot that is known for being customer-oriented!"

"The local bookstore I own hosted a competition that asked students that type of promotional events they like to see. "Pay Only for Sales" Viral Network Marketing

We all know word-of-mouth advertising is the most effective way to get new customers into your store. Our Viral Network Marketing (VNM) lets you pay your loyal customers to refer their others to buy at your store instead of throwing money away with ineffective marketing campaigns. NVM lets your business pay their loyal customers only when a saleis made by a friend who they referred, meaning you can gauge and track your expenditures and create flexible discount structure to award both the referrer and the referee.

"As a new lawyer in town, I was struggling to get new clients. I used the NVM system to reward my loyal clients for referring new clients to use my services. The new clients were given a discount, my old clients were paid, and it all cost less than any other type of marketing platform. Since then, my practice has boomed and I started to turn away new clients!"
- John, HHK Law

"When my heating oil service company started selling alarm systems, we use the VNM system to pay our existing customers to spread the word for us. In no time my company became the local market leader in alarm systems. It was a no-brainer...word-of-mouth advertising works!"
-Bill, Meenan Oil Co.

Recruit New Talent

Competitions can also be used as a more effective and reliable way to evaluate new candidates for positions in your company. Simply host a competition that asks candidates to solve a unique or frequent problem at your company and make the award a job or internship!
