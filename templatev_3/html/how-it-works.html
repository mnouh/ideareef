What's a Reef?

A reef is a community of customers centered around businesses to co-create and co-develop new products and services.

1
Join Us!

When you create your profile you will automatically be assigned your hometown and school networks.

2
Follow Your Favorite Companies and Friends

3
Get Active and Get Rewarded

By solving competitions, voting for your favorite ideas, and referring your friends to check out new businesses, you receive badges that earn you more prizes and notoriety in your school and community, as well as on IdeaReef world.
