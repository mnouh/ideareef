Empowering Businesses and Communities with Ideas & Networks

Solve Competitions

Solve competitions and vote for your favorite ideas in competition hosted by the businesses you love. Showcase your skills, earn badges, and compete with other users from your school and around the world.

Refer Friends

Tell your friends to check out your favorite bars, restaurants and other stores to earn money every time they buy there.

Get Rewarded

Earn cash, discounts, free prizes, even jobs and internships just by posting your ideas and using your network. Get recognized by your university and your community for your great ideas!

Every time you win, your school and hometown win too. Schools and cities will be able to compare and compete how active their members are in their local and national communities.
